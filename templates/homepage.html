Banking Recommendation System

Mortgage

Debt instrument secured by the collateral of a real estate property, that the borrower is obliged to pay back with a predetermined set of payments.

Credit Card

Enables cardholders to make purchases on credit. It comes with a pre-set credit limit which users can utilise to make purchases effortlessly.

Savings Account

  • Savings Builder : Great for regular savers, with a balance under £10,000.
  • Instant Saver : Flexible saving without the need to deposit regularly
  • Premium Saver : Better suited for those with balances over £25,000.

Overdraft

Allows account holder to continue withdrawing money even when the account has no funds in it or has insufficient funds to cover the amount of the withdrawal.
